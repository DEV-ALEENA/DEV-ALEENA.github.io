Roseanne Park

Roseanne Park (born 11 February 1997), better known by the stage name Rosé (Korean: 로제), is a New Zealand singer currently based in South Korea. Rosé signed with South Korean label YG Entertainment following an audition in 2012, training there for four years. She eventually made her debut as a vocalist in the girl group Blackpink in August 2016
